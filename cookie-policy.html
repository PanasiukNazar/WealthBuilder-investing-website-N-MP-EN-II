Cookie Usage in Manas Webinars

At LeanneManas, we are committed to ensuring that your experience with our webinars, including our Financial Planning and Investment Help Manas sessions, is both informative and personalized. To enhance your experience, we use cookies and other tracking technologies during our Manas webinars.

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device while you participate in our webinars. These cookies help us remember your preferences, improve your experience, and ensure that the webinar content is tailored to your needs. They also allow us to track how users interact with the Manas Webinars, enabling us to improve the event for future participants.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies to improve your experience during the Manas webinars:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the functionality of the webinar platform and cannot be turned off. They enable basic features such as logging in and maintaining session security.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us track and analyze how participants interact with the webinar content. This allows us to improve our presentations and services.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences, such as language settings, to provide a more personalized experience during the webinars.

Consent to Cookie Usage

By participating in the Manas Webinars, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect your ability to participate in all features of our webinars.

If you choose to disable cookies, please be aware that some parts of the webinar experience may not function properly, and certain personalized features may not be available.

More Information

If you have any questions about how we use cookies in our Manas webinars, please feel free to reach out to us. Our team at LeanneManas is committed to ensuring a transparent and secure experience for all participants.
